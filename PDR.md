# PRODUCT REQUIREMENT

# DOCUMENT FOR LUXEL

# Problem

Travelers often face fragmented booking experiences across multiple platforms, unclear pricing,
and overwhelming interfaces. Many travel websites feel outdated, transactional, and
impersonal.

Luxel aims to solve this by offering a unified, simple, intuitive, and visually refined platform that
allows users to discover, plan, and book travel confidently in one place.

## Goals & Objectives

### Primary Goals

- Provide a seamless end-to-end travel booking experience
- Establish Luxel as a premium, trustworthy travel brand
- Simplify complex travel decisions through clear UI and content hierarchy

### Success Metrics (KPIs)

- Increased booking conversion rate
- Reduced bounce rate on landing and booking pages
- Average time to complete a booking
- User satisfaction and repeat visits

## Target Users

### Primary User Persona

- **User Type:** Leisure & business travelers
- **Age Range:** 22 – 45
- **Behavior:** Mobile-first, digitally savvy, values convenience and aesthetics
- **Key Needs:** Speed, transparency, trust, inspiration and simplicity
- **Pain Points:** Confusing booking flows, hidden fees, information overload


### Secondary Users

- Travel planners & tour enthusiasts
- Corporate or group travelers

## Core Features & Functional Requirements

### Flight Booking

**Description:** Users can search, compare, and book flights.

**Key UI Requirements:**

- Search form (From, To, Dates, Passengers, Class)
- Results with filters (price, duration, airline)
- Clear price breakdown
- Call-to-action: **“Book Flight”**

### Hotel Reservations

**Description:** Users can discover and book hotels globally.

**Key UI Requirements:**

- Search by destination, date, guests
- Hotel cards with images, ratings, amenities, price/night
- Detail page with gallery, reviews
- Call-to-action: “Reserve Hotel”

### Car Rentals

**Description:** Users can book rental cars at destinations.

**Key UI Requirements:**

- Pick-up & drop-off location and date
- Vehicle cards (type, capacity, transmission)
- Pricing per day
- Call-to-action: **“Rent now”**


### Travel Tour Highlights (Yearly)

**Description:** Curated travel experiences and tours promoted for the year.

**Key UI Requirements:**

- Highlighted destinations and tour packages
- Seasonal collections (e.g. Summer Escapes, Winter Getaways)
- Large visual cards with storytelling copy
- Call-to-action: “Explore Tour”

## Core Pages & Screens

- Homepage (Search + Inspiration)
- Flight Booking Page
- Hotel Booking Page
- Car Rental Page
- Tour Highlights Page
- Booking Review & Checkout
- User Account / My Trips
- About Luxel
- Contact & Support
- Error & Empty States

## User Flows

### Primary Booking Flow

1. User lands on homepage
2. Selects service (Flight / Hotel / Car / Tour)
3. Searches and filters results
4. Views details
5. Completes booking
6. Receives confirmation feedback

## UI/UX Principles


- **Premium minimalism:** Clean layouts with strong visual hierarchy
- **Trust-first design:** Clear pricing, reviews, and reassurance cues
- **Inspiration-driven:** Use imagery to spark travel desire
- **Efficiency:** Fewer steps, clear CTAs
- **Consistency:** Unified components across all booking types

## Visual Design Requirements

### Brand Feel

- Modern, luxurious, calm, aspirational and simple

### Typography

- Elegant primary font for headings
- Highly readable body font
- Clear hierarchy across pages

### Color Palette

- Primary luxury brand color
- Soft neutrals for backgrounds
- Accent colors for CTAs and status states

### UI Components

- Search bars
- Filter panels
- Cards (flights, hotels, cars, tours)
- Buttons & CTAs
- Modals & alerts
- Navigation (header & footer)

## Interaction & Motion

- Subtle hover states on cards and buttons
- Loading states for searches
- Smooth page transitions
- Micro-animations for booking confirmation

Motion should enhance clarity, not distract.


## Responsiveness & Platforms

- Fully responsive (Mobile, Tablet, Desktop)
- Mobile-first booking experience
- Touch-friendly controls
